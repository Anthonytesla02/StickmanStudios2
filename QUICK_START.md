## Step 1: Create the Workflow (One-Time Setup)

1. Look for **Workflows** in your workspace sidebar
2. Click **+ New Workflow**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: `Start application`
   - **Task Type**: Execute Shell Command
   - **Command**: `npm run dev`
4. Click **Save**

---

## Step 2: Start the App

1. Click **Run** on your new workflow
2. Wait 10-15 seconds for the server to start
3. You'll see: `serving on port 5000`

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Video

1. Open your app (it'll show in the Replit webview)
2. Click **"Try Creator"** or navigate to `/creator`
3. Paste this test script:

```
A stickman wakes up and stretches
He walks to the kitchen
The stickman makes breakfast
He sits down to eat happily
```

4. Click **"Generate Video"**
5. Watch the progress bar (takes ~20-30 seconds)
6. Download your video when complete!
